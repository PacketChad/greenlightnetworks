The Small Business Challenge

Congrats, you did it!  You made the challenging decision to be your own boss and open a small business.  Any why not?  You're good at what you and you want to share it with your community, and possibly the world!

Beyond the challenges you'll face with the aspects of your business you understand, you'll inevitably be faced with technology questions, costs, and decisions that you may have little to no idea about.  

That's where we come in.  You tell us what your business needs to do, and we'll work with you to put technology to work for you.  That leaves you more time to focus on what's important for you.

Our Services

User Enablement

We will work with you to select the right hardware and software solutions that will enable your staff to work efficiently with the flexibility your business needs.  This includes device management and clear acceptable use policies.

Network Infrastructure

We will design and implement both wired and wireless network infrastructure to enable your business to connect to the Internet, cloud services, and each other.

Security

We will design and implement best practice security solutions that protect your users, assets, data, and locations from both internal and external threats.

Assurance

We will validate all solutions work with your business processes, staff know how to use the solutions, and recommend improvements to improve productivity.

All design documentation is provided.

Multimedia

We will design and implement conference room and common space media solutions.

Cloud

We will work with you to select the right cloud and SaaS solutions for your business, and connect you to them securely from anywhere you need.

About Us

  • Overview
Green Light Networks is an IT Services consulting organization that started in 2008 offering cost-effective open source solutions for small businesses.

Since its inception, Green Light Networks has helped small businesses without dedicated IT staff utilize technology to run, and grown their business with minimal capital or operational expenses.
